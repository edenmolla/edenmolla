The time Machine


The Time Machine is a science fiction novella
by H. G. Wells, published in 1895 and written
as a frame narrative. The work is generally
credited with the popularization of the concept
of time travel by using a vehicle or device to
travel purposely and selectively forward or
backward through time.

Ender's Game
